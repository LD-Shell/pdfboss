About this tool

Delete Pages from a PDF — Free, Fast & Private

Got a blank page at the end? An outdated cover? A section you don't need? Just click the pages you want to remove, or type the page numbers, and save a clean copy without them.

Click or Type

Click page thumbnails to select them visually, or type page numbers and ranges (like "1, 3, 5-10") for precise control.

Odd & Even Shortcuts

One-click buttons to select all odd or even pages — useful for removing blank backs from double-sided scans.

Completely Private

Your document stays on your computer. No uploads, no servers — the pages are removed right in your browser.

How to delete pages from a PDF

  1. 1

    Open your PDF

    Click "Select PDF File" or drag your document onto the page. Every page appears as a visual thumbnail.

  2. 2

    Select the pages to remove

    Click page thumbnails to mark them for deletion (they'll get highlighted). Or type page numbers in the input box. Use the Odd/Even buttons for bulk selection.

  3. 3

    Delete and save

    Click "Delete & Save" and a new PDF without the selected pages downloads to your device. Your original file stays unchanged.

Frequently asked questions

Does it modify my original file?

No. A new PDF is created without the deleted pages. Your original file on your device is untouched.

Can I delete a range of pages at once?

Yes. Type ranges like "5-10" to delete pages 5 through 10, or mix individual pages and ranges like "1, 3, 5-10".

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything happens in your browser. Your document never leaves your device.

Is it free?

Completely free — no account, no watermarks, no limits.
