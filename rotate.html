About this tool

Rotate, Reorder & Organize PDF Pages — Free

Got pages that scanned upside down? Need to rearrange a report before sending it? This tool gives you a visual page editor — drag to reorder, click to rotate, and delete anything you don't need. All without leaving your browser.

Drag to Reorder

Every page shows as a thumbnail card. Drag them into the right order — no guessing, no typing page numbers.

Rotate & Delete

Fix upside-down or sideways scans with one click. Select multiple pages and rotate or delete them all at once.

Completely Private

Your document stays on your computer. No uploads, no cloud — the organizing happens right in your browser.

How to organize PDF pages

  1. 1

    Open your PDF

    Click "Select PDF File" or drag your document onto the page. Every page renders as a visual thumbnail.

  2. 2

    Drag, rotate, or delete

    Drag page cards to reorder them. Click the checkbox to select pages, then use the rotate or delete buttons in the toolbar. Shift-click to select a range of pages quickly.

  3. 3

    Save the organized PDF

    Click "Save PDF" and your reorganized document downloads instantly. You can also extract selected pages into a separate file.

Frequently asked questions

Can I select multiple pages at once?

Yes. Click the checkbox on individual pages, or Shift-click to select a range. Then rotate, delete, or extract the entire selection with one click.

Can I extract pages into a separate file?

Yes. Select the pages you want, then click "Extract." A new PDF with just those pages downloads separately — your original remains unchanged.

Will the quality change?

No. Pages are copied directly from the original — no re-rendering or compression. The output is identical in quality.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything happens in your browser. Your document never leaves your device.

Is it free?

Completely free — no account, no watermarks, no limits.
