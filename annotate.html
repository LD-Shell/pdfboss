About this tool

Mark Up Any PDF — Free, Private & Easy

Whether you're reviewing a contract, studying for an exam, or leaving feedback on a report, this tool lets you highlight, underline, draw, and add notes directly on any PDF. No account needed, no file uploads — everything stays on your computer.

Five Annotation Tools

Highlight, underline, strikethrough, text notes, and freehand drawing — everything you need to mark up a document for review or study.

Permanently Embedded

When you save, your annotations are baked into the document. The recipient can see them but can't edit or remove them — great for official feedback.

Completely Private

Your file never leaves your computer. No uploads, no cloud servers, no tracking. Safe for confidential documents.

How to annotate a PDF

  1. 1

    Open your document

    Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop. It loads instantly — nothing is uploaded.

  2. 2

    Pick a tool and color

    Choose from highlight, underline, strikethrough, text note, or freehand draw. Pick any color from the palette.

  3. 3

    Draw on the page

    Click and drag to highlight or underline an area. Click to place a text note. Use the freehand tool to draw arrows, circles, or anything else. Navigate between pages using the thumbnails below.

  4. 4

    Save and share

    Click "Save Annotated PDF" and your marked-up document downloads instantly. Share it by email, upload it, or print it.

Frequently asked questions

Can the recipient edit or remove my annotations?

No. When you save, the annotations are permanently embedded into the page. They can't be moved, deleted, or edited by anyone — the document is essentially "flattened" with your markup baked in.

Is my file safe?

Yes. Your file never leaves your computer — there's no upload, no cloud storage, and nobody else can see it. Everything happens right in your browser.

Is it free?

Completely free — no account, no watermarks, no limits on how many PDFs you can annotate.

Can I undo my changes?

Yes — before saving. Hover over any annotation and click the X to remove it, or click Undo to remove the last one. You can also clear all annotations from a page or the entire document. Once you click Save, the annotations become permanent.

Can I add text notes anywhere on the page?

Yes. Select the "Text Note" tool, click anywhere on the page, and start typing. Press Enter to save the note, or Shift+Enter for a new line. The text will be embedded at that exact position in the final PDF.

Will the annotated PDF print correctly?

Yes. The saved PDF opens and prints normally in any viewer. Highlights, underlines, drawings, and text notes will all appear exactly as you placed them.
