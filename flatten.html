About this tool

Flatten PDF Forms — Lock Fields Permanently

Filled out a PDF form and want to make sure nobody can change your answers? Flattening converts all the interactive fields — text boxes, checkboxes, dropdowns — into permanent, uneditable content. What you typed becomes part of the page itself.

Permanent & Tamper-Proof

Once flattened, the form fields are gone for good. Nobody can alter, clear, or re-fill what you entered — it's locked into the page.

Looks the Same

The document looks identical before and after flattening. The only difference is the fields can no longer be clicked or edited.

Completely Private

Your document stays on your computer. No uploads, no cloud — the flattening happens right in your browser.

Frequently asked questions

What does "flatten" actually mean?

A fillable PDF has interactive form fields sitting on top of the page — text boxes, checkboxes, dropdowns. "Flattening" merges those fields into the page itself, so the values you entered become permanent, uneditable content. It's like printing the form and scanning it back — except the quality stays perfect.

Can someone undo the flattening?

No. Once the form fields are flattened, they're gone permanently. The values become part of the page and can't be changed, extracted, or re-activated.

Is my file uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your document never leaves your device.

Is it free?

Completely free — no account, no watermarks, no limits.
