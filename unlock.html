Frequently asked questions

Yes. This tool removes protection after you enter the correct password. It does not crack, guess, or bypass unknown passwords. It's designed for documents you own but have restrictions on.

No. Your password is used once in your browser to decrypt the file, then it's gone. Nothing is logged, stored, or sent to any server.
